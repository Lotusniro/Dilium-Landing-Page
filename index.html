On the Job Maintenance

Anyone will be able to solve malfunctions of your product, without needing training or having to rely on support teams: simply aim at the product, the visor will automatically detect the defect and provide simple and precise instructions to the wearer on how to fix it.

Spatial Plan

Anyone will be able to inspect a site or area by physically moving within it. Without the need for specific training or relying on support teams, it will be possible to make annotations or extract real-time 3D modeling acquired through the scanning of the entire area or small portions of it.

Spatial Support

With Spatial Support, you can access remote assistance from experienced experts who can see what you see and provide annotated guidance and human-to-human interaction , anywhere at any time.

  • Share your Point of View using the main camera.
  • Easily understand QR codes and Barcodes.
  • Allow Remote Specialists to view through a web browser.
