Applications areas

Training and Onboarding

On the job Maintenance

Anyone will be able to solve malfunctions of your product, without needing training or having to rely on support teams: simply aim at the product, the visor will automatically detect the defect and provide simple and precise instructions to the wearer on how to fix it. Now available also for Vision Pro with object recognition.

Learn More

Spatial Plan

Anyone will be able to inspect a site or area by physically moving within it. Without the need for specific training or relying on support teams, it will be possible to make annotations or extract real-time 3D modeling acquired through the scanning of the entire area or small portions of it. The collected data will be sent remotely and can be used for maintenance, design, or analysis, both in real-time and later on.

Learn More

Spatial Support

With Spatial Support, you can access remote assistance from experienced experts who can see what you see and provide annotated guidance and human-to-human interaction, anywhere at any time. Share your Point of View using the main camera. Easily understand QR codes and Barcodes. Allow Remote Specialists to view through a web browser.

Spatial Assistance

Imagine an empty ChatGPT filled with your custom and private information (we import your xls, pdf, docs, website, etc.). Available in Spatial Computing or as an API-as-a-Service solution (e.g., for a chatbot), you can interact with your personal agent and ask about your company’s processes. For onboarding new staff, daily operations, maintenance, safety, and training, you can save time by simply asking your agent.
